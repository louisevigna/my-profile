Swimming

I practiced competitive artistic swimming for 10 years in Le Pecq. I started this sport at 7 years old, completely unaware that is was going to become such an important thing in my life. Training every week for 10 years, 5 to 10 hours a week with the same teammates who became my sisters, was such a fulfilling experience. Being captain of my team for 4 years, from 2018 to 2022 taught me perseverance, organization, empathy and patience. I had the repsonsibility of at the same time motivating my team but also briunging the best out of every girl. Knowing their assets and perfecting them. I am very passionate about this sport for so many reasons. The swimming pool became my second home, feeling the water on my skin was comforting and my teammates helped me grow as a person.

Makeup can make you feel more empowered and confident because you took time to mke yourself look good. Makeup makes me feel like myself. I reveal my identity through makeuo. Languages

I fluently speak english, french and spanish. French being my native language, I learned english from having english-speaking babysitters when I was a child. Studying in a bilingual school helped me perfect my english. However, I discovered my passion for languages thanks to my high school spanish teacher who made me fluent in spanish. I learnt spanish in two years and aspire to learn many more in the following years. Speaking a language allows to feel closer to the person speaking in front of you. Even though english in the common language, it is different speaking the native language of a country. For instance, when i spoke spanish in Spain last summer, I felt instantly closer to the spanish person in front of me. Some bond creates itself and this feeling is very fulfilling to me. The culture that comes with the language is also very interesting and rewarding.
